## 0.0.1

- Initial release
- Pure Dart SVG avatar generation
- Flutter widget (`Beanhead`) for easy integration
- 17 customizable dimensions: skin tone, eyes, eyebrows, mouth, hair, facial hair, clothing, accessories, graphics, hats, body type, and color options
- Random avatar generation with `BeanheadConfig.random()`
- Based on [BeanHeads](https://github.com/RobertBroersma/beanheads) by Robert Broersma
